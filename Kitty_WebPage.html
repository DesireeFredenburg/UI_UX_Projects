Kitty Lovin Time

A kitten is a juvenile cat. After being born, kittens display
primary altriciality and are totally dependent on their mother
for survival. They do not normally open their eyes until after
seven to ten days. After about two weeks, kittens quickly
develop and begin to explore the world outside the nest.
